# Can't Out Astra The Machina (Astra Machina – FTC 16010) | Kuek - 16010

This is the CAD for **Can't Out Astra The Machina**, built by **FTC Team 16010 Astra Machina** during the 2025–2026 *DECODE* season. The full robot is viewable and downloadable in the link Fusion published below.

## Drivetrain

This robot's drivetrain features a standard parallel plate drivetrain with a dead-axle design for high power efficiency along with the drivetrain being geared up from 435 RPM to ~500 RPM (17:15) ratio for the perfect balance of both speed and torque for this season's game.

## Intake

This robot features a 4-stages of compliant wheels to intake balls into the robot powered by 2x GoBilda 6000 RPM motors on a 3:1 ratio for a total output RPM of 2000. The initial three stages of the intake are powered by the first motor with the last stage being powered by the additional motor for a quick transfer time.

## Shooter + Turret

This robot's shooter features an FRC-inspired flywheel along with multiple sets of counter rollers to reduce backspin on the artifacts when launched to reduce bounce outs from the goal. This is all powered by 2x GoBilda 6000 RPM motors.

This robot's turret is mounted onto an X-Contact bearing supported by a 1/4" carbon fiber plate with M4 screws surrounding it perimeter to secure it in place. This is all powered by 2x Axon Max Servos powered on a 1:1 ratio for quick adjustments needed during the match when scoring artifacts while encountering defence.

## Advantages
- Power-efficient dead-axle PPDT Design
- Quick intake pick ups and quick transfer.
- Consistent shooting from all areas of field
